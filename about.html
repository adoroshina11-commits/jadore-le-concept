The Concept

About the
Concept.

My first introduction to fashion came long before I understood the industry itself. It lived in conversations, sketches, fabrics, and stories passed down by my mother, who studied fashion and taught me to look closely.

Growing up among five siblings and a family of entrepreneurs, I learned to adapt, create, and approach the world with curiosity. Business taught me structure; art taught me observation.

I collect passions the way others collect objects. Photography, writing, jewelry making, design, fashion, and culture all offer different ways of telling the same story. This space is a reflection of that curiosity — an archive of moments, ideas, and details that might otherwise be forgotten.
